Frequently Asked Questions

Is there an age limit?
Nightmare Vermont is recommended for ages 13 and up. It is a high-startle, high-gore event. We do not recommend it for young children. Parental discretion is advised.
Is the event scary?
Yes, that's the point! We use a combination of live actors, special effects, tight spaces, darkness, and loud noises to create a terrifying experience.
What is the "Extra Bloody" show?
Our final show on November 1st is a special, messier version of the event. Expect to get wet and covered in stage blood and other goo. We recommend wearing clothes you don't mind getting stained, or bringing a poncho. It's a fan favorite!
Is the event accessible?
The main path of the haunted house is wheelchair accessible. However, please be aware that it involves navigating dark and sometimes narrow corridors. Strobe lights and fog effects are in use. Please contact us in advance if you have specific accessibility concerns.
Can I buy tickets at the door?
We strongly recommend buying tickets online in advance. We sell out every year, and it is highly unlikely there will be tickets available for purchase at the door.
